The Story Behind LaRedoux

Blending intimate acoustic textures, intricate multi-part live looping techniques, and distinct vocals, LaRedoux has steadily grown a dedicated global following. Based near Montreal, Canada, the collaborative synergy between Redvers and Mélissa defines their discography.

From whimsical, morning-inspired tunes like "Eggs and Toast" to deep indie compositions found across their full-length albums like If You Want to Fly, You've Got to Let Go and Misty History, their music remains raw, human, and authentic.
