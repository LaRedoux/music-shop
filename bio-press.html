The Story Behind LaRedoux

Camille Bowie, performing under the moniker LaRedoux (and known earlier in her career as Redvers), found her footing in music during her childhood in Gloucester, England. Spending her lunch hours writing songs in her school's music room, she developed an early, intimate bond with the piano and acoustic guitar. That initial spark set off a nomadic musical journey that would span decades and take her through more than 20 countries before she ultimately settled down and found a home in Trois-Rivières, Quebec.

Operating as LaRedoux, Camille has carved out a unique, deeply personal corner of the internet by prioritizing the raw "live experience" over sterile studio polish. She deliberately avoids the heavily edited, pristine traps of traditional recording environments in favor of unscripted, spontaneous performance. On her digital stage, she builds her music piece-by-piece in real time using a guitar and a loop pedal. Honest, vulnerable originals like "Giving It Up," "Naked," and "Everything Starts With A Dream" invite the audience straight into her creative process, stripping away the usual barriers between artist and listener.

Now a fixture in the regional music scene, she balances local festival slots—including appearances at FestiVoix de Trois-Rivières—and radio spots with regular broadcasts on Twitch and YouTube. Her sets are entirely interactive, creating a genuine communal space where a global fanbase can request classic covers from bands like The Beatles and The Who, discuss her gear setup, or just hang out. As she continues to evolve, even weaving French-language tracks into her sets alongside her native English, LaRedoux keeps her focus exactly where it belongs: on grounded, human storytelling.

Press Release

Ditching the Studio: LaRedoux Brings Raw Live Music and Unscripted Looping to a Global Stage

TROIS-RIVIÈRES, QC – FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Following recent festival slots and radio appearances, independent singer-songwriter LaRedoux (Camille Bowie) is proving that great music doesn't need a slick recording studio to cut through the noise. Based in Trois-Rivières, Quebec, the UK-born artist has taken her decades-long journey through the global music scene and brought it into a vibrant, interactive streaming space where tracks are built from the ground up, live and unedited.

Performing solo with a guitar and a loop station, LaRedoux avoids the clean traps of modern production to keep the focus purely on raw songwriting and spontaneous connection. Her sets slide naturally between intimate acoustic numbers and layered, groove-driven arrangements, featuring originals like "Hounded By Ghosts" and "Serendipity" alongside impromptu covers of classics from The Beatles and The Who. It is a straightforward, honest approach to music-making that relies entirely on performance instincts rather than studio wizardry.

What truly sets LaRedoux apart is how she transforms the solitary act of watching a broadcast into a shared, communal room. Viewers are invited to shape the evening's setlist, talk shop about gear, and share in the genuine post-gig exhaustion that follows a real performance. "Everything starts with a dream," Camille notes—and her current work is a testament to keeping that dream independent and entirely human. To catch upcoming stream schedules or dive into her live catalog, visit her Discord community or her official YouTube and Twitch channels.
