The Story Behind LaRedoux

Camille Bowie, performing under the moniker LaRedoux (and known earlier in her career as Redvers), found her footing in music during her childhood in Gloucester, England. Spending her lunch hours writing songs in her school's music room, she developed an early, intimate bond with the piano and acoustic guitar. That initial spark set off a nomadic musical journey that would span decades and take her through more than 20 countries before she ultimately settled down and found a home in Trois-Rivières, Quebec.

Operating as LaRedoux, Camille has carved out a unique, deeply personal corner of the internet by pairing high-fidelity audio quality with unscripted, spontaneous performance. Instead of relying on post-production edits or studio fixes, she lets her technical skill take center stage, building pristine, multi-instrumental arrangements piece-by-piece in real time using a guitar and a loop pedal. Honest, vulnerable originals like "Giving It Up," "Naked," and "Everything Starts With A Dream" invite the audience straight into her creative process, blending a polished sonic experience with a genuinely human connection.

Now a fixture in the regional music scene, she balances local festival slots - including appearances at FestiVoix de Trois-Rivières, Scène de la terrasse à l'Amphithéâtre Cogeco, and radio spots - with regular broadcasts on Twitch and YouTube. Her sets are entirely interactive, creating a genuine communal space where a global fanbase can request classic covers from bands like The Beatles, Kylie Minogue and The Who, discuss her gear setup, or just hang out. As she continues to evolve, even weaving French-language tracks into her sets alongside her native English, LaRedoux keeps her focus exactly where it belongs: on grounded, high-quality storytelling.

Press Release

Studio-Grade Sound Meets Live Energy: LaRedoux Brings High-Fidelity Indie-Folk to a Global Stage

TROIS-RIVIÈRES, QC – FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Following recent festival slots and radio appearances, independent singer-songwriter LaRedoux (Camille Bowie) is proving that delivering pristine audio quality doesn't require being locked away in a traditional recording studio. Based in Trois-Rivières, Quebec, the UK-born artist has taken her decades-long journey through the global music scene and brought it into a vibrant, interactive streaming space where studio-grade tracks are built from the ground up, completely live.

Performing solo with a guitar and a loop station, LaRedoux relies on sharp performance instincts and a finely tuned live setup to keep the sonic quality exceptionally high. Her sets slide naturally between intimate acoustic numbers and layered, groove-driven arrangements, featuring originals like "Hounded By Ghosts" and "Serendipity" alongside impromptu covers of classics from The Beatles and The Who. It is an honest, transparent approach to music-making that delivers a polished, full-band experience without the need for post-production editing.

What truly sets LaRedoux apart is how she transforms the solitary act of watching a broadcast into a shared, communal room while maintaining excellent production value. Viewers are invited to shape the evening's setlist, talk shop about gear, and share in the genuine post-gig energy that follows a real performance. "Everything starts with a dream," Camille notes—and her current work is a testament to keeping that dream independent, sonically sharp, and entirely human. To catch upcoming stream schedules or dive into her live catalog, visit her Discord community or her official YouTube and Twitch channels.
